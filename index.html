About PitchSense AI

PitchSense AI uses cutting-edge computer vision and AI to analyze pitcher mechanics, providing actionable insights to prevent injuries and enhance performance. Our mission is to keep athletes healthier, extend careers, and improve the overall safety of the game.

Key Features

AI-Powered Motion Tracking

Real-time pose estimation and analysis of pitcher arm angles, shoulder, and elbow stress.

Predictive Injury Model

Uses historical and real-time data to predict potential injury risks, allowing proactive rest and training adjustments.

Custom Performance Reports

Detailed, data-driven reports tailored to each pitcher, helping coaches optimize performance while minimizing injury risk.
